Keyboard operations:
1. Press J/j, K/k, and L/l to change the location of the light. The light is at the yellow sphere.
2. Press P/p, Y/y, and R/r to change the angle of the camera. Press B/b, N/n, and M/m to change the coordinate of the camera.
3. Press W/A/S/D to move object parallel to the X Z surface. Press SPACE to toggle the current object to be moved. Press U/u to move object along the Y axis.
